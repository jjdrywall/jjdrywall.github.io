Meet the Owners of JJ Drywall

At JJ Drywall, we’re a family-owned business led by a dedicated father-son team, proud to serve Austin and the surrounding cities with top-quality work at an affordable price.

Jose, the founder of JJ Drywall, started the company in 2016 with a passion for dependable, detail-driven craftsmanship. Since then, he has built over 200 homes for various companies and clients, earning a reputation for reliability and excellence in the field.

Jocin, Jose’s son, joined the business two years ago and brings fresh energy and commitment to every project.

Together, they combine years of experience with a hands-on approach to ensure each job meets the highest standards.

JJ Drywall specializes in drywall installation, tape and float, and painting for both new home construction and remodels. Whether you're starting from scratch or refreshing your space, you can count on us for honest work, expert results, and a team that treats your project like their own.
